Our process

How Ecom Heroes finds the top 1% of talent.

Step 01

Global
Talent Hunt

We actively source talent from across the globe to find experienced e-commerce operators, marketers, creatives, and support specialists with proven results.

Step 02

Experience
Verification

Every candidate is carefully reviewed to verify past work experience, platform expertise, communication skills, and real-world performance in e-commerce and agency environments.

Step 03

Deep Skill
Assessment

Candidates complete role-specific evaluations designed to test their technical abilities, problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and ability to perform under pressure.

Step 04

Communication &
Culture Screening

We identify professionals who communicate clearly, work proactively, and integrate seamlessly into fast-moving e-commerce brands and agencies.

Step 05

E-commerce
Systems Training

Selected candidates go through additional training on e-commerce workflows, tools, SOPs, team collaboration, and performance expectations to ensure they're fully prepared to operate at a high level.

Step 06

Top 1%
Hero Selection

We filter out the weak, inexperienced, and unreliable applicants and move forward with only the top 1% of talent who meet the Ecom Heroes standard.

Step 07

Hero
Placement

We match your business with the perfect Ecom Hero based on your goals, systems, team structure, and operational needs, so you can scale with confidence and without the overhead of traditional hiring.
